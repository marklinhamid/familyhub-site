FamilyHub — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

FamilyHub (“the app”) is a family-organiser app published by AIMAILABS. This policy explains what data the app handles. Plain version: your family data lives on your device, and the optional AI features only send data when you turn them on.

Data stored on your device

Everything you enter — family members, events, children’s details, bills, groceries, receipts, meal plans, vaccination records, photos and your settings — is stored locally on your device in the app’s private storage. We do not have a server that collects or holds this data, and it is not uploaded anywhere by default.

Photos & camera

The app can use your camera or photo library so you can attach a member photo, a child’s ID photo, or a receipt image. These images are copied into the app’s private storage on your device.

Optional AI features

The receipt scanner, school-timetable scanner, meal planner and AI assistant are optional. When (and only when) they are enabled and used, the relevant content — a receipt or timetable photo, your meal preferences, or a short summary of your app data — is sent over a secure (HTTPS) connection through our service to our AI provider, Anthropic (Claude), to generate a result. That content is processed to return your result and is handled under Anthropic’s terms. Our service records only anonymous usage counts (no personal data) for rate-limiting and cost control. If you never enable these features, no data leaves your device.

Ads

The free (Basic) version of the app shows banner advertising provided by Google AdMob. To serve ads, AdMob may collect a device advertising identifier and limited device and usage information, as described in Google’s advertising policies (policies.google.com/technologies/ads). Subscribing to the Pro plan removes ads.

Notifications

The app schedules local reminder notifications on your device. These are generated on-device and are not sent to any server.

Purchases

If you subscribe or make a purchase, the transaction is handled by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Data we do not collect

We do not use third-party analytics SDKs, we do not run our own servers that collect your family data, and we do not sell your data. (The free version does show ads via Google AdMob — see “Ads” above.)

Children

The app is intended for parents/guardians to organise family life. It is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your control

You can delete any record in the app at any time, and uninstalling the app removes all locally-stored data.
